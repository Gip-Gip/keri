What's Κερί?

Κερί, pronounced keri, is a small AM transmitter project that aims to be a simple, small and usefull tool for testing and demo-ing old recievers, due to the lack of AM stations in many areas. It is the first transmitter to use a comparator-based design, and (at least plans to be) competitively priced at an affordable ~$45. Κερί's advantages include:

  • Easily upgradable design
  • No need for old, expensive or custom components
  • Schematic, PCB and BOM files under the Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License
  • Simple and straighforward operation
  • USB-based power for portablility, if need be
